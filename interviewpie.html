  Interview With Pie  
 
 

We have conducted a two way interview with our beloved senior artist and Co-Founder of GDD, Mr. Mohd Syafia a.k.a Pie. He has shown a lot of efforts and talent around the GDD project. Here goes the interview :

GDD : So Syafie, or should I call you Pie? How is your day?

Pie : Yeah sure, you can call me Pie. As usual, a bad day.

GDD : How so?

Pie : It's a cold day and I feel sleepy.

GDD : I'm sure it is, Pie. Tell us about how you work your ideas.

Pie : My ideas usually come from machines, weird organisms and stuffed composition. Then I would take my favourite pencil and sketch my ideas out. Usually I will start with the eyes and then the head. Up until everything is finished.

GDD : How would you tell if your work is finished?

Pie : It usually depends when I'm drawing. I usually pick up more ideas when I draw, so I don't really stop when they are flowing instantiously.

GDD : What type of artist do you consider to be your influence?

Pie : Artist who have humour values in their works such as Zint from Gempakstarz and figure drawing by Puppeteer also from Gempakstarz.

GDD : Thank you Pie for your time.

During the interview, Pie have generously provided us with a process of his artwork 'Steam' which is also uploaded in our first exhibition.
