Exhibition Brief
1. Freestyle
December 13, 2011

Every few months we release a digital exhibition of art, photography and music. We decide on a theme for all of us to ponder, develop and work on, helping each other to create out best interpretations of that theme.

Our current exhibition is 1. Freestyle. For our first exhibition, we have collected over 26 works from all of our artists. Our members have experimented with their mediums and produced a set of beautiful illustrations, photography and traditional mediums.

This is the first exhibit to be launched in the new website we setup, thanks to the hard work and dedication of Fix, Thaq and Pie. As this exhibit is being released, people are already anticipating and enjoying these works and meeting some of the artists who created them.

Mohd Syafie is our featured artist this time around, producing awesome doodle and 3D illustrations. Capek Torres, Blur, Najib and many more created stunning graphic illustrations, while Fix and Pojan produced cool 2D vector work.

- Ajim Jimkimjat (GDD Staff)
